This deliciously spicy salad is full of flavor and texture. Sure to pack a crunch (and perfect for when your in a crunch), this quick meal will leave you feeling refreshed, energized, and ready to take on your day!

Photo Credit: Wholesome Kitchen

Ingredientes

  • 1 bag of mixed greens
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 tomatos
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 belle pepper (any color)
  • 1 fresh squeezed lemon
  • 1 fresh squeezed lime
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • A dash of cayenne pepper
  • A sprinkle of Slap Ya Mama's seasoning

Time to make your ensalada!

  1. Dice your tomatos, onions, and cucumbers
  2. Cut your lemon and lime in half, set aside one half of each for later
  3. Squeeze the lemon and lime over the chopped vegetales
  4. Wash and season your mixed greens
  5. Rinse your mixed greens
  6. Grab the other halves of your lemon and lime to squeeze over the mixed greens
  7. Drizzle your olive oil and toss everything together
  8. Season your salad to taste
  9. Refrigerate if you like cold salad, or eat right on the spot!
